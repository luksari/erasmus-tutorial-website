Steps

during exchange

  • When you are starting new semester go to local international office to take necessary documents
  • Fill them all and take your new student card, student number and confirmation date of starting your mobility
  • Fill your english Learning Agreements with all eventualy changes
  • Go to your local coordinator to accept all changes and give it back to office
  • Wait patiently for all signs (it can took few days)
  • Send all pappers to Poland
  • Enjoy your best adventure of life and start crying before end of semester
  • You can buy "ESN card" to have some benefits during mobility and cheaper flights in Ryanair!!
  • Pass your all exams, do all projects
  • Don't forget about your confirmation of ending your mobility and go back home
